Senegambian Jollof Rice 🍚

Jollof rice aka Benachin (pronouns Ben-a-chin) is a traditional Gambian dish that has become a worthy variety in West African cuisines.

It is made up of rice, vegetables, and meat that have all been cooked together.

Let's start cooking!🍴

Ingredient list (Serving: 2 people):

  • ½ lb boneless beef (diced)
  • 3 cups of rice
  • 4 onions (peeled and diced)​
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Bay leaves
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 hot peppers
  • 3 tsp tomato puree
  • 1 ½ cups vegetable oil
  • 2 bell peppers (sliced)
  • 1 large eggplant (chopped)
  • ½ small cabbage (chopped)​
  • 6 cups of water
  • Salt to taste​
  • Black pepper​
  • Vinegar​

Instructions:

  1. Put the beef pieces into a bowl.
  2. Season the meats with salt, black pepper, garlic and a dash of vinegar, then leave undisturbed for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat a large pot, add the vegetable oil and fry the beef until it is brown
  4. Add the onions to the beef and cook until brown.
  5. Add the tomatoes and the tomato puree together with the minced hot pepper.
  6. Stir these ingredients together and cook for 15 minutes.
  7. Pour in the water and bring to a boil. Add the chicken to the pot.
  8. Add the chopped cabbage, chopped eggplant and the bay leaves to the pot.
  9. Sprinkle salt to taste.
  10. Use a large spoon to dish out the vegetables and then set them aside
  11. Rinse the raw rice in cold water, then add to the pot. Add the sliced bell peppers and bring to a boil.
  12. Reduce the heat and let the ingredients simmer.
  13. When the liquid has been absorbed and the rice is tender, turn off the heat and place in a serving dish. Arrange the vegetables on top of the rice and beef. Serve hot
